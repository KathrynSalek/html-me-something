Focus and Development

I'm a data driven marketer and customer engagement advocate. I believe great marketing serves the consumer by providing them the most relevant information about products and services that they want and/or need. Great marketing also establishes a connection and trust with those it serves. More and more technology is the vehicle for connecting to and serving consumers.

Completing the LaunchCode curriculm will help me to be an even more technically skilled at understanding and contributing to cutting edge digital consumer experiences. On the front end, I will be able to better lead advanced site experiences that dynamically change based on the visitor's prior experience with the brand. On the back-end, I will be able to better lead the data collection, stitching and analysis efforts that supply the information to fuel superior experiences.
